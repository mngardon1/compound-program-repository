Phase 2 · Overview

Package

With tested positioning in place, Phase 2 builds the conversion mechanism — the assets that turn "this is the right person and the right problem" into a paying client.

The Conversion Mechanism

Positioning told you who you're for. Package shows them how it works.

By the end of Phase 1, your positioning is sharp enough that the right person reads your work and recognizes themselves. That's necessary. It's not sufficient. They still need to see a clear path from "this is my situation" to "this is how I'd work with you" before they take action.

Phase 2 builds that path. Two assets:

  • → Offer Doc (internal): the protocol your business runs on. Forces every hard commercial decision and feeds every downstream asset.
  • → Sales Page (external): the buyer-facing translation of the Offer Doc. The thing that takes a positioned prospect from problem-aware to ready-to-buy.

Both are built using the Offer Architect skill in your Strategist Coach — already installed back in 0.2. Phase 2 is where you start using it.

Positioning is the upstream layer. Package is where the upstream work converts into revenue. Skip this and your positioning lives only on your website.

Prerequisite Check

Phase 2 only works if Phase 1 is solid.

Before you start Phase 2, confirm:

  • ✓ Your 7 Phase 1 positioning files are saved in your /Compound Consultant OS folder and uploaded to your Strategist Coach project
  • ✓ You've completed at least 5 ICP conversations testing your positioning
  • ✓ You've iterated your positioning at least once against real market signal (1.4)
  • ✓ Your positioning.md is at v1.1 or higher (iterated at least once)

If any of these is missing, return to Phase 1 first. The Offer Architect skill reads your positioning files at session start and will refuse to build on vague inputs — saving you a session of wasted work.
